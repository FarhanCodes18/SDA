Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Are the courses self-paced or live?

We offer a hybrid learning model containing pre-recorded class libraries for self-learning, as well as live notices, live problem-solving sessions, and interactive workshops weekly.

How can I claim my certificate?

Once enrolled in a course, navigate to the Certificate tab in your Student Dashboard. Complete the verified verification details form, process the token fee using Razorpay, and your certificate will be sent directly via WhatsApp and Email.

Is there a placement guarantee?

While we provide extensive interview prep and direct recruitment referrals to our 100+ hiring partners, placement ultimately depends on your performance in the technical rounds.
